Ideal candidates will help with a new implementation for a HCM, UKG, to add a new component for employee benefits management for the organization. Project is to build integrations to benefit providers (e.g. medical, dental, life) to automate changes based on employee election changes, life events, etc. Additionally, this project will help build out wizards so employees can self-select during open enrollment and life events.

Main Responsibilities:

1. Identify all of the needs UKG will have in terms of policy, procedure, and detailed requirements.

2. Work with HR to produce or compile all the necessary documentation and artifacts

3. Obtain sign off by HR

4. Provide back to UKG for execution

Requirements:

-Strong BA Experience, minimum 3-5 years

-3-5 years experience working with HR and benefits


Nice to have but not required: UKG experience and familiarity with their benefits admin solution.
